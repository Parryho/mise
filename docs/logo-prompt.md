# Mise Logo — Generierungs-Prompts

## Prompt 1: App-Icon (nur M)

```
Minimalist geometric monogram letter "M" logo mark.

Construction: The M is built from three separate vertical modules with equal gaps between them, connected by a flat horizontal bar at the top. Orthogonal design — strictly 90-degree angles, no diagonal lines, no curves except minimal corner rounding. The top closes flat (no pointed peaks). All strokes have identical constant width. The three modules are clearly separated with visible gaps.

Style: Flat design. No shadows, no gradients, no 3D effects. No decorative elements. Pure geometry.

Proportions: Perfect 1:1 square format. The M fills approximately 70% of the canvas, centered with generous padding (safe zone for mobile app icon masking).

Color: Warm off-white M (#F0EDE8) on deep graphite background (#141414). Not pure black, not pure white — subtle warmth in both.

Mood: Infrastructure. Stability. Precision. Like a building's blueprint — not decorative, but engineered. Should feel like a system logo, not a startup logo. Timeless, not trendy.

Do NOT include: text, taglines, decorative elements, shadows, gradients, bright colors, rounded/playful shapes, checkmarks, cooking imagery, food icons.
```

## Prompt 2: Vollständiges Logo (M + Schriftzug)

```
Minimalist logo combining a geometric monogram "M" symbol with the wordmark "Mise" and tagline "before serve".

Symbol: Abstract M built from three separated vertical modules connected by a flat top bar. Orthogonal (90° only), constant stroke width, flat top closure, no pointed peaks. Minimal corner rounding matching the typography.

Layout: Symbol left, "Mise" right (capital M, lowercase "ise"). Below or next to it in smaller size: "before serve" in lightweight type. Clean spacing — gap between symbol and text equals the x-height of the lowercase letters.

Typography: Clean geometric sans-serif for "Mise". Lighter weight for "before serve". No serif, no script, no decorative fonts.

Color: Warm off-white (#F0EDE8) on deep graphite (#141414).

Style: Flat. No shadows, no gradients. Mathematical precision. Feels like a professional kitchen management system — authoritative yet approachable.

Do NOT include: cooking icons, chef hats, food imagery, multiple colors, playful elements, 3D effects.
```

## Prompt 3: Variante für Figma/Vektor-Tools

```
Design a geometric monogram "M" on a 12x12 unit grid.

Specifications:
- Stroke width: 2 units (constant everywhere)
- Gap between elements: 1 unit
- Safe zone padding: 15% of total canvas
- Vertical centering: offset +2% upward (optical correction)
- Corner radius: minimal, identical on all corners
- Top: flat horizontal bar connecting all three vertical stems
- No diagonal lines — purely orthogonal (90° angles only)
- Three clearly separated vertical modules

The M should read as both:
1. The letter M (for "Mise")
2. Three abstract checkmarks/modules (representing structure, control, workflow)

Color: monochrome — light mark on dark ground
Format: 1:1 square
Style: flat, no effects
```

## Kontext für Designer / AI

Falls zusätzlicher Kontext nötig:

> Mise ist ein Küchen-Management-Tool für die Gastronomie. Der Name kommt von "Mise en place" (Vorbereitung). Die Marke steht für Struktur, Ruhe und Kontrolle — damit Köche wieder kochen können statt im Büro zu sitzen. Das Logo soll wirken wie Infrastruktur: stabil, zeitlos, mathematisch präzise, aber nicht kalt. Autoritär und zugänglich zugleich.
